Privacy Policy - Jokim Tyres

Introduction

At Jokim Tyres, your privacy is of utmost importance. This policy outlines the information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to protect it.

Information Collection

We collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, and shipping address when you place an order. We also gather non-personal information like browsing behavior and IP addresses for analytics purposes.

Use of Information

The personal data we collect is used to process orders, improve our services, and communicate with you about promotions and new products.

Cookies and Tracking Technology

We use cookies to enhance your user experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser, but please note that disabling cookies may affect the website's functionality.

Data Security

We implement secure protocols to protect your data. While no system is entirely foolproof, we make every effort to secure your personal information.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you'd like to exercise your rights, please contact us at any time.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date. Please review this page periodically.
